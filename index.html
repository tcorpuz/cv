Steel North Shore (SNS) and Steel South Auckland (SSA) are wholly New Zealand owned stockists of steel products. Our major point of difference is that we will cut steel to length, no cutting job is too big or too small – cutting steel to length is our business. We take pride in the high calibre of our staff some of whom have over 40 years of experience in the industry and who have a passion for steel and its benefits. We aim to make business easy for our customers and to achieve outstanding results for them. We encourage your enquiries and we welcome account and cash sale customers. Cash, Eftpos, Visa and Mastercard facilities are available.
